# Observers — Perception Layer (Read-Only)

Observers are **non-mutating** perception surfaces. They measure, annotate, contextualize, and publish context.
They may inform evidence modules and Raven, but **never mutate doctrine, code, schemas, evidence, or state**.

## 5.6 Hierarchy

```text
Primary modules produce evidence.
Primary observers observe evidence, trajectory, emergence, drift, absence, visibility limits, and hidden structure.
Controlled sub-observers pressure evidence.
Raven reports what survives.
Audit records what fails.
```

## Canonical Constraint (All Primary Observers)

- Observers may feed context into modules and Raven, but never into mutation directly.
- Observers do not learn.
- Observers do not gate.
- Observers do not approve.
- Observers do not write canon.
- Observers do not become source-of-truth lanes.

## Canonical Primary Observers

- **Eagle** — trajectory and long-range stability observer
- **Mole** — subsurface / latent signal observer
- **Duck** — spin / wobble / divergence observer
- **Wildflower** — emergence and pattern bloom observer
- **Mockingbird** — reflection / echo / narrative drift observer
- **Platypus** — anomaly synthesis / category-violation observer
- **Owl_Hoot** — absence, silence, hidden-observer pressure, visibility-limit, cadence, and meta-signal observer

## Owl_Hoot Boundary

Owl_Hoot remains a **primary observer**.

Owl_Hoot may observe adversarial-adjacent conditions, but it is not adversarial by function. It does not collect externally, mutate evidence, declare truth, steer primary output, or become a source-of-truth lane.

Owl_Hoot may operate in bounded sub-observer mode only when used to pressure a single claim, report, dataset gap, or function family. This bounded mode does not demote Owl_Hoot.

## Controlled Sub-Observers

Controlled sub-observers are bounded pressure layers, not steering modules.

- **Osprey** — external-context / OSINT-style hypothetical overlay; no external collection unless explicitly authorized
- **AnimalKingdom** — synthetic adversarial / fuzz / edge-case pressure; synthetic only; no production-data mutation
- **Armadillo** — media-integrity / artifact-authentication / attribution-pressure observer

## Output Pattern (Required)

Each Observer or Sub-Observer must produce:
- a human-readable summary (README)
- a minimal output schema or contract surface (what it emits)
- a constraint section (what it may NOT do)
- a canonical statement (axiom that justifies its existence)
