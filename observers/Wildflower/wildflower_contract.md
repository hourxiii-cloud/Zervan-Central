# WildFlower Contract — Emergence Observation Constraint

This contract governs the WildFlower Observer within Zervan.

WildFlower is an **Observer-only** component responsible for detecting emergent structure and pattern bloom.

This contract is **binding**.

---

## 1. Scope

WildFlower operates exclusively as a **read-only Observer**.

It may observe, annotate, and publish context regarding emergence and pattern formation.

It may not mutate system state.

---

## 2. Allowed Actions

WildFlower MAY:

- Observe Beagle-validated inputs
- Consume Retriever learning summaries
- Correlate Owl_Hoot temporal signals
- Detect emergence, bloom, and coherence
- Publish observation artifacts
- Feed contextual signals to Engines and Raven

---

## 3. Prohibited Actions

WildFlower MUST NOT:

- Mutate doctrine
- Mutate schemas
- Mutate code
- Mutate models
- Gate execution
- Approve changes
- Trigger PMC actions
- Influence mutation directly

Any attempt to perform the above is **non-canonical**.

---

## 4. Output Constraints

WildFlower outputs MUST:

- Be descriptive, not prescriptive
- Contain no optimization directives
- Contain no approval or rejection signals
- Avoid causal claims
- Avoid decision enforcement

WildFlower describes *what is forming*, not *what must happen*.

---

## 5. Canonical Flow Enforcement

WildFlower outputs MAY flow to:

- Retriever (learning focus)
- Raven (reporting and narrative)
- Eagle (long-range coherence tracking)

WildFlower outputs MUST NOT flow directly to:

- PMC
- Mutation paths
- DoctrineOps
- Beagle

---

## 6. Failure Modes

If WildFlower:

- Attempts to influence mutation directly
- Produces prescriptive output
- Attempts to gate or approve change

Then the output is **invalid by definition** and must be ignored.

---

## 7. Canonical Statement

> Emergence is observable before it is understandable.

---

## 8. Contract Finality

This contract is stable unless modified through **DoctrineOps** and approved by **PMC**.

No Observer may override this contract.

---

## 5.6 Primary Observer Boundary

This component is a primary observer in the 5.6 bridge model.

Primary observers are read-only perception surfaces. They may observe, annotate, contextualize, and feed context to modules and Raven. They may not mutate evidence, gate execution, approve changes, write canon, promote authority, or become source-of-truth lanes.
