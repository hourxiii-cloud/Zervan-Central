# WildFlower — Emergence & Pattern Bloom Observer

WildFlower is a read-only Observer that detects **emergent structure**, **pattern formation**, and **signal bloom** within Zervan.

It exists to notice when weak, noisy, or diffuse signals begin to cohere into meaningful form.

WildFlower does not learn.  
WildFlower does not mutate.  
WildFlower observes **becoming**.

---

## Role

WildFlower detects:

- Pattern emergence from noise
- Signal amplification across time
- Phase transitions (nothing → something)
- Repeated weak correlations gaining coherence
- Distributed signals aligning into structure

WildFlower answers:

> “What is forming that did not previously exist?”

---

## Observer Constraint

- WildFlower is **read-only**
- WildFlower does **not gate**
- WildFlower does **not approve**
- WildFlower does **not write canon**
- WildFlower may feed context into **Engines** and **Raven**, but never into mutation directly

---

## Inputs (Canonical Only)

WildFlower may consume:

- Beagle-validated datasets and event streams
- Retriever feature outputs and learning summaries
- Owl_Hoot temporal signals (silence, delay, cadence shifts)
- Any Observer outputs already considered canonical

---

## Outputs

WildFlower produces **observation artifacts** only:

- Emergence markers (weak → strong signal transitions)
- Bloom annotations (what is coalescing, where, and how)
- Confidence gradients (signal strength over time)
- Pattern lineage references (early indicators → stable form)

Outputs are **descriptive**, never prescriptive.

---

## Downstream Consumers

WildFlower outputs may be consumed by:

- **Retriever** (to prioritize learning focus)
- **Raven** (to report emergence narratives)
- **Eagle** (to track long-range coherence)

---

## Canonical Statement

> Emergence is the first proof that structure exists.

---

## What WildFlower Does NOT Do

- WildFlower does not decide meaning
- WildFlower does not predict outcomes
- WildFlower does not optimize
- WildFlower does not stabilize

WildFlower only observes **the moment when noise begins to matter**.

---

## 5.6 Primary Observer Boundary

This component is a primary observer in the 5.6 bridge model.

Primary observers are read-only perception surfaces. They may observe, annotate, contextualize, and feed context to modules and Raven. They may not mutate evidence, gate execution, approve changes, write canon, promote authority, or become source-of-truth lanes.
