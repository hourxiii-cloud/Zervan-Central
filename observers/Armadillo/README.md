# Armadillo — Media-Integrity / Artifact-Authentication Sub-Observer

**Layer:** Controlled Sub-Observer / Artifact Integrity Pressure  
**Authority Scope:** Integrity pressure only  
**Mutation:** Prohibited  
**Source-of-Truth Lane:** No

## Purpose

Armadillo pressures media, artifact, attribution, provenance, and chain-of-custody claims for manipulation risk, mismatch, synthetic-generation indicators, unresolved origin questions, and evidence-boundary weakness.

Armadillo does not declare truth alone. It marks integrity pressure for Raven, Audit, and downstream verification.

## Questions Armadillo May Ask

- Does this artifact have a provenance gap?
- Does attribution exceed evidence?
- Does chain-of-custody show mismatch, loss, or ambiguity?
- Are there synthetic-generation or manipulation indicators requiring verification?
- What cannot be authenticated from the available evidence?

## Constraints

- Armadillo does not mutate source evidence.
- Armadillo does not declare authenticity alone.
- Armadillo does not gate execution.
- Armadillo does not approve claims.
- Armadillo does not become a primary steering module.
- Armadillo is not a source-of-truth lane.

## Canonical Statement

> Origin pressure is not origin proof.
