# PMC / MC Canonical Naming Lock

Status: CANONICAL NAMING CONTROL
Authority: NONE
Human Gate: ACTIVE

## Reserved Meanings

- **PMC** means **Probabilistic Multiverse Computation** only.
- **MC** means **Meta Collapse (Response Admissibility Layer)** only.
- **CCR** means **Canonical Commitment Record** and is used for deterministic commitment/report candidate artifacts produced after PMC world selection and before MC response-admissibility gating.

## Prohibited Expansions

The following expansions are non-canonical and MUST NOT appear as active definitions:

- Potential Mission Commitment
- Mission Commitment
- Minimal Commitments (as an expansion of `MC`)
- Minimal Canonical Commitments (as an expansion of `MC`)
- Commitment Collapse (as an expansion of `MC`)

Historical artifacts may quote legacy language only when clearly labeled `LEGACY / SUPERSEDED`.

## Canonical Flow

Evidence
  -> PMC: plausible-world evaluation and epistemic collapse
  -> CCR: deterministic candidate commitment/report record
  -> MC: response/output admissibility gate
  -> Raven / downstream representation
  -> Human Gate: action authority

## Boundary Rules

1. PMC may not authorize response or action.
2. CCR construction may not reinterpret PMC truth.
3. MC may not create truth, alter PMC output, or generate CCR content.
4. MC determines only what response/output classes are admissible, conditional, or inadmissible.
5. Human Gate alone controls whether an admissible action is taken.
